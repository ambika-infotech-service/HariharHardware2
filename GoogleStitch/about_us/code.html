Our Story

Building foundations and coloring dreams for over two decades.

Founded in 2002, Harihar Hardware & Paints began with a simple vision: to provide the local community with the highest quality building materials and expert advice. What started as a modest neighborhood shop has evolved into a cornerstone of the regional construction industry.

For over 20 years, we have stood by our commitment to reliability. We understand that every nail, every beam, and every drop of paint contributes to someone's dream home or a business's new beginning. That's why we only partner with the world's leading brands to ensure your projects stand the test of time.
