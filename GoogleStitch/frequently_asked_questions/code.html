Ordering & Delivery

How long does delivery take? expand_more
Delivery typically takes 2-4 business days within the local area. For orders outside our standard zone, it may take up to 7 business days depending on your location and the weight of the items.
What are the delivery charges? expand_more
We offer free delivery for orders over $500. For orders below this amount, a flat rate of $25 is applied for local deliveries. Bulk orders (over 500kg) may require special quotation.
Can I track my order status? expand_more
Yes! Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also view your order history and status by logging into your account dashboard.

Products & Brands

Which paint brands do you stock? expand_more
We are authorized dealers for Asian Paints, Berger, Nerolac, and Dulux. We stock a complete range of interior, exterior, and specialized industrial coatings from these premium manufacturers.
Do you offer custom color mixing? expand_more
Absolutely! Our in-store computerized tinting machines can match any color sample you bring. We can mix over 10,000 shades instantly while you wait.
Are your hardware tools covered by warranty? expand_more
All power tools come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty. Hand tools are covered against manufacturing defects. Please keep your original invoice to claim warranty services.

Services

Do you provide professional painting services? expand_more
Yes, we have a network of certified professional painters. We offer end-to-end painting solutions including wall inspection, site preparation, and post-painting cleanup.
Can someone help with site measurement? expand_more
We offer free site visits for material estimation within city limits. Our technicians will measure your space and provide a detailed quote for required paint and hardware.
